# Problem Statement

## Existing Problem

In many organizations, change requests are managed manually using emails, spreadsheets, or paper-based records. This process is time-consuming, difficult to track, and lacks transparency. It often results in delayed approvals, poor communication, and inefficient management of change requests.

## Proposed Solution

The Change Management System provides a centralized web-based platform where users can submit change requests, monitor their status, and communicate with developers. The system improves transparency, simplifies the approval process, and ensures efficient management of change requests.

## Expected Outcome

- Faster processing of change requests.
- Better communication between users and developers.
- Improved transparency and accountability.
- Organized tracking of all change requests.
- Reduced manual effort and paperwork.
